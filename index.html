Our Story

Born from a love of genuine hospitality

Saveur was founded in 2019 by Chef Michel Laurent — a graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris — with a singular vision: to bring genuine fine dining to Jakarta. We source directly from trusted local farms and artisan producers.

Our team of 18 passionate chefs transform each ingredient into an experience that transcends the plate — celebrating culture, season, and story.
